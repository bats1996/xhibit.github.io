Patricia Beja

BA (Hons) Illustration and Visual Media
London College of Communication

Patricia’s creative adventure started during her A-Level’s where she studied Visual Arts in Lisbon, Portugal, which taught her various disciplines such as painting, printmaking, design, etc. This newfound passion in art encouraged the young artist to pursue a BA (Hons) Illustration and Visual Media at the University of the Arts London, England. Through this degree she discovered the world of animation. She has entered several competitions such as the TFL Elizabeth Line competition and the Glastonbury Festival x WaterAid, both ending successfully. The artist’s obsession with colour became a distinct aesthetic in the work she produces which helps her produce different sensations on the human eye. Patricia likes to creatively and sensitively deal with contemporary issues such as climate issues, human rights, music and cultures/subcultures.
