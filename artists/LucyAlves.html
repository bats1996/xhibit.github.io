Lucy Alves

BA Fine Art: Photography
Camberwell College of Arts

A triptych of prints exploring the value of an artist’s work and labour. Each image shows the intrinsic value of the material and labour costs, displaying a digitally drawn amount of £146.09. The digital media explores the commercialisation of art and the bright colours gives a gentle nod to the pop art movement. As an emerging artist, it is hard to remove oneself from the process of making work to sell, this piece highlights this notion as well as questioning ideas around pricing art.
