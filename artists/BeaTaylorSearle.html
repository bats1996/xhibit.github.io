Bea Taylor Searle

BA Fine Art (with creative computing)
Central Saint Martins

Bea Taylor Searle is an multidisciplinary artist working between the digital and physical space through sculptures, performative websites and text-based art. The creative process of dissecting the browser allows her to critically investigate our relationship to it. She explores the cursor as an extension of the body and how the internet has become part of our domestic space. Her research focuses on how the viewer interacts with the space in the browser and if this space makes the viewer a user.
