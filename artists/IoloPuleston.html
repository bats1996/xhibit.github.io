Self-portrait with dog is a painting I did. It is of me with a dog by my side. To me, the painting represents catharsis. I used one brush and lots of oil paint. I am a self-taught painter and I think my naivety gives this painting a feeling of innocence and genuineness. In the painting, I have overcome a sense of loneliness and feel happywith a friend by my side. I hope something in my painting makes you happy
